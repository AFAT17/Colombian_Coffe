Colombian Coffe

1Is Colombian Coffee The Best In The World?s

Traditionally the cafe of Colombia has proudly raised the title of "best coffee in the world". Today the competition is tough and other coffees struggle to occupy his throne, or at least share it: coffee Jamaica Blue Mountain, cultivated in the "blue mountains" of Jamaica, coffee Indonesia Java, Ethiopia Moka Lim or Costa Rica Tarrazú.

2What Makes Colombian Coffee So Good?

The composition of the soil and its quality is a determining factor to achieve an excellent grain and coffee flavor and aroma supreme. In Colombia it is grown in volcanic soil, with a lot of nutrients, in addition, In Colombia the cultivation of coffee is a matter of national pride. Colombian coffee growers strive for their plantations, exude love for their work and strive at every step.

Colombian Coffe Brands

Juan Valdez

Juan Valdez, besides being one of the most recognized brands in the world, is a company that works for the economic, social and environmental sustainability of coffee families and the country. Since its creation in 2002, Juan Valdez has worked intensively on projects involving women and young coffee growers, people with disabilities and of course in making visible and recognizing the quality of Colombian coffee.

Cafe Quindío

Café Quindío S.A.S. is a coffee roasting company with 30 years of experience and pioneer in the development of coffee products in Quindío, Colombia. In this department we harvest cherries enriched and classified with special techniques that have made Quindío Coffee a brand with prestige in the palates of coffee connoisseurs.
